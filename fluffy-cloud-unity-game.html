Fluffy Cloud

Embark on a whimsical adventure in Fluffy Cloud, a captivating 2D mobile game developed using Unity and C#, where you navigate through enchanting worlds, utilizing lightning-fast reflexes to conquer challenges and discover hidden treasures.

Project Overview

Embarking on the creation of Fluffy Cloud, a captivating 2D mobile game developed using Unity Engine and C#, I eagerly sought to enhance my programming skills and explore the fascinating world of game development. Throughout this solo endeavor, I delved into the realm of object-oriented programming and immersed myself in Unity's versatile features.

As part of my journey, I studied various AI algorithms, including A* and alpha-beta, with the intention of incorporating them into the game design, hoping to introduce a new level of sophistication. However, upon careful consideration, I realized that these strategies were not well-suited for the gameplay experience I envisioned. Instead, I made the bold decision to innovate by implementing a lightning feature that dynamically tracks the cloud's position, injecting a fresh and exhilarating challenge into the game.

By embarking on this project, I not only honed my programming skills but also pushed myself to explore new territories and overcome the hurdles of creating something unique.

While the game is not currently available on app stores, I have developed a fully functional version as an APK file. Although I decided not to publish it due to my desire to further refine the graphic design of certain elements, I am open to sharing the game and its source code privately upon request. This allows me to demonstrate my programming skills and showcase the effort I have put into creating an engaging and visually appealing mobile game experience.
